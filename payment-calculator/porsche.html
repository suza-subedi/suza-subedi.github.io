Overview

When the Porsche 959 first came out in 1986, it was the fastest car in the world, it was a technological marvel, packed with space age features, unreal power, tight handling, and the Porsche Standard in refinement, the 959 really was the most, advanced car in its time, and still quite impressive today. It was one of the first high-performance cars in the world that Had an all-wheel drive system and it set up the building blocks for the legendary legacy of the all-wheel drive 911s until now.
-- Some of the Features involved include :
- The 959 was originally designed as a rally car not a sports car, and it holds some of that DNA still as it won the Paris-Dakar Rally
- A 0-60mph time of 3.6 seconds
- When released it was the fastest car in the world at 197mph (317kmph) becoming the first car in the world to achieve speeds of above 190 mph.
- It was the first production car ever to have tire pressure sensors.
