Overview

$2,900,000




The E-Type cemented the sporting legacy of Jaguar when it was released, and it is one of the most iconic cars in existence to this date, beloved by many collectors, the E-Type screams british racing legacy and pure class with some of the most elegant looks on 4 wheels in Decades. The E-Type established itself as a light weight race machine, with some models being completey stripped of any comforts and being raw track machines, where as others were sold with the finest leather interiors to excude the most opulant looks on the road.
Some of the features involved include :
- The car was designed by aircraft engineers thus creating that beautifully aerodynamic exterior.
- A 0-60mph time of 7.6 seconds
- The car almost didnt make it to its own launch at the Geneva Motor Show, and was driven all the way from the Factory in the UK to the Show in Switzerland.
- There were 18 "lightweight" versions built, becoming a legend in the british racing scene being one of the most dominant cars to ever compete.
